Tips To Make Perfect Desserts

  • Use milk for moist bakes

    • Even the most beautiful looking cake can be ruined by a dry sponge. Add a splash of milk to the batter after you’ve folded in the flour.

  • Add flavour with brown butter

    • Try substituting it into bakes that call for melted butter or oil.

  • Avoid soggy bottoms

    • If you’re blind-baking a pastry case for a sweet tart, brush with egg white five minutes before its cooking time is up.

  • Create a double boiler

    • It will heat your chocolate without a risk of uneven heating and potential burns.

  • Delay removing from oven

    • When you are finished baking your dessert, do not take it out of the oven right away. Let the heat gradually escape.

  • Use corn oil instead of butter

    • Use corn oil which can stand up to high heat baking and has a neutral flavor for tasty desserts.

  • Baking with stevia

    • It’s as simple as replacing sugar with stevia for all your dessert recipes and we’re talking tons of calories spared, but same sweet taste.
