The D'Foodie Story

Our starting point was not so smooth as people expected. We started off by baking some recipes from Google, Tiktok, & Instagram. After few years of following people's recipe, we started baking with our own recipes for fun and that was the starting point for us to think that we should develop a website using our homemade recipes soon. However, there were so many try & errors, giveups, and thoughts that maybe we had put our hopes too high. Through thick and thin, we finally made our first ever own recipe, a simple croissant. Since then, we made many more recipes and that was how D'Foodie was ebstalished. We built our own website on 21th November 2021 and our recipes start to get shared more among people around January 2022 and it was a great chance to be able to share our recipes with a lot of people. If you have any interesting recipes, contact us here to submit one, and stand a chance to get your recipe to be featured on our website!
